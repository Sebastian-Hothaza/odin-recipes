Chocolate

Description

This delicious recipe will have you licking your fingers in no time!

Just make sure to share; after all, that's half the fun!

Ingredients

  • Cocoa
  • Brown Sugar
  • Milk
  • Love
  • Care

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients in bowl
  2. Tell a bed time story and let them hang out in fridge overnight. Do NOT wake overnight.
  3. Form shapes and place on baking sheet
  4. Bake at 425 Farenheit for 5-6 hours.
  5. Start again, did you really think they needed 5-6 hours of baking?!
